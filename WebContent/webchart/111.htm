1        Scope

1.1   This Policy provides guidance to all employees based in the offices out of Mainland China. This policy is intended to supplement the iClick Group Travel and Entertainment Reimbursement Policy. Except where any local Policy is more restrictive than the iClick Group policy, conflicts must be resolved in favor of the iClick Group policy. Please consult the iClick Group policy for additional guidance.
